Our Story

Inspired by reading the original notebooks and papers of Newton, Da Vinci, Einstein and more, Atoms to Astronauts was launched in May 2021 by Chemistry teacher, Damian to produce science and maths gifts that are made with sustainable materials and practices and are of the highest quality.

How our notebooks are made

Handmade by a team of experts using techniques developed over generations, our notebooks are made by a small independent manufacturer in England.
